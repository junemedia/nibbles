Enter Contests With Short Deadlines

 

Most people tend to procrastinate. If they can wait until the last minute, they will do so. Therefore, a contest that has a short period to enter will disqualify any Johnny-come-lately types. This will give you less competition, which means a greater chance of winning.

 

Another tip is to enter contests occurring during holidays and summer vacation time when families are busy with relatives and festivities. Also, since a postal increase came recently, double the number of entries you would normally send. The rate change may be enough justification for some possible entrants to not "waste their money on a contest."
