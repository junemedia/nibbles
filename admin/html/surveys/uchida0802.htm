Craft Survey

This poll is being done for a well-known craft manufacturer who is developing a new line of craft products. If you are a crafter who would like to provide input into product development then we would appreciate your participation. Below you'll find some questions, each highlighting a different subject. The questions are easy and all about crafting! It should take you no time at all! Just click the appropriate button to answer each question. Thank you for your help!



1. Which is the best description of you as a crafter?

Passionate Crafter: shop for crafts weekly, spend free time at craft shows, or make lots of projects in all types of crafts.
Dedicated Crafter: specialize in several areas of crafting and dabble in others; crafting is major use of free time; or I shop for crafts several times a month.
Craft Lover: shop for crafts regularly; I have several projects going at once; or I wish I had more time for crafting.
Craft Dabbler: look at finished items, collect craft projects and supplies and think, “I could do that sometime”; or occasionally I do a project.
Home Craft Decorator: use craft supplies to decorate my home, furniture and accessories.
Professional Crafter: make crafts primarily to sell or to teach a class.
