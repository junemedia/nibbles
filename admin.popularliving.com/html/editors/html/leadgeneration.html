Offers are promoted in relevant newsletters and via solo email to demographically targeted subscribers. Each week Ampere Media LLC promotes and collects 50,000 qualified client leads.

 

Ampere Media uses a 100% opt-in, non-incentive process. This means the prospect must read and purposely select an offer. The prospect is NOT rewarded with points, sweepstakes entry, or cash back for selecting a client offer on our site.

 

Step 1a –Convey Additional Information

 

Some clients need to convey additional details about an offer to the prospect. Ampere Media makes this easy by providing an “additional information” link within the offer. The prospect clicks on the “additional information” link and reads more about the offer.

 

Step 2 –Personal Data Collection From The Lead:

 

When incorrect information is entered our system automatically flags the data and requests that the information be corrected.  In the above case the state of New York does not match the city or the zip code.

 

Some of the quality control checks include:

 

  • City, State & Zip code match.
  • Verified and deliverable postal address.
  • Area code matches zip code.
  • Prefix is a geographic match to the city.

 

Ampere Media uses over 80 proprietary bounds check systems to review and scrub the lead data before being sent to the client.

Step 4 – Lead Delivery To The Client:

 

New scrubbed and CASS certified leads are sent to clients via email every business day. This enables the client to import the lead electronically to their data systems. This saves time, eliminates order entry error, and enables the client to contact the lead within 24 hours of request for information.

 

Lead data files are usually sent in an Excel “.csv” format. However Ampere Media can send leads in whatever file format the client needs.

 

Step 5 – Immediate Contact With The Lead:

 

Finally Ampere Media can send a confirmation or  “auto email” to the consumer immediately after the information request.  

 

Conclusion & Unique Advantages of Online Marketing:

 

·   Creative can be changed any time; the Client can adjust the offer as needed.

·   Less than 24 hours elapse from when the offer is requested and the clients receive the lead data. This is quicker than direct mail and costs less than inbound telemarketing.

·   Clients receive only CASS certified, qualified, opt-in leads. Clients know their
outgoing mail will be delivered to a verified postal address.

 

·   The client can send a “thank you” and specific follow-up information within seconds. The sales process is well underway by the time the client receives the lead data.

 

Online marketing is a new media for a client to add to their promotional mix. This increases
the resources available to bring in new business. Like all direct response media, online marketing requires testing to optimize best performance.
