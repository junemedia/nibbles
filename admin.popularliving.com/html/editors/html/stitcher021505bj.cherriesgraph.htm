When you print out the graph, turn the 'Cherries left side graph' 90 degrees left, add the 'Cherries right side graph' turned 90 degrees left next to it.  When you join the two graphs, that is the middle of the pattern.  

Use the one long 'Cherries Graph' for the Hand Towel.  To make the Pot Holder and the Oven Mitt, use either the 'Cherries left side graph' or the 'Cherries right side graph'
