Tip Corner:


Tip #1:  Before using DecoColor™Glitter paint marker on ceramic, metallic or glass surfaces, clean the surface with soap and water or wipe it down with rubbing alcohol to remove dirt and oil.

Tip #2:  Don’t be alarmed if your heat tool smokes when you first use your new heat tool. It is the result of residual oil within the mechanism. It should clear up in a few minutes. If the smoking persists with each use, then return it right away to where it was purchased or contact the manufacturer.

Tip #3:  Add texture to fabric with the Fabric Marker by using plastic wrap. Crinkle plastic wrap and open it back up again. Paint on the plastic wrap with desired fabric marker color. Use a spritzer of water to blend some of the colors. Then lay your tee-shirt on top of the color. Make sure to put a cardboard between the tee-shirt so the colors won’t bleed to the back.

Tip #4:  To avoid damaging or ruining projects, always test product on an inconspicuous area first. This will save a lot of time and effort.

Q: How would I find decorations to go onto candles?  They have prongs and attach to the outside of the candles.  Have seen them called candle jewels etc. but have had no luck finding anything on the net.  Thanks.
