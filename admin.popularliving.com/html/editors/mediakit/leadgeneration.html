Qualified Opt-In Lead Generation 

 

Quality Leads That Work!

 

Ampere Media specializes in providing opt-in business and consumer leads. Leads come from prospects that respond to our national partners and advertising campaigns.   Mostly utilized for 2-step marketing campaigns, we gather complete lead data including name, e-mail, and mailing address.  Unique offer-specific data is also gathered as needed.  Our program is commonly used for developing qualified house files, telemarketing call centers, and MLM networks.

  • Leads are 100% opt-in and self-selecting.
  • Over 80 quality control checks are used in evaluating lead validity.
  • We are the DIRECT lead source – and not a lead aggregator.
  • Leads are EXCLUSIVE to each client.  Leads are NOT re-sold.
  • Standard lead data includes; complete name, email, address and phone number.
  • All leads are postal (CASS) certified.
  • Additional client-specific questions are also available.
  • Auto reply emails are also available.

Client Industries include:

  • Health Insurance
  • Life Insurance
  • Business Opportunity
  • Education/Distance Learning Programs
  • Mortgage and Finance Offers
  • Magazines and Catalogs
  • General Consumer Offers
